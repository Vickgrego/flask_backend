3) Now, right click on any request in the Name section and select "save as HAR with content".

What is HAR and why we need this? HAR is basically a copy of all your requests. When you report a bug, sometimes, when it's not unclear what API call caused a bug, it's better to provide to a developer a har file, so the developer can analyze all you network activity.
